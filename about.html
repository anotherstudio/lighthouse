Lighthouse, founded by sibling duo — Priya and Anjali Gaekwar, is a strategic brand transformation agency based in Mumbai, India.
Ours is a passion-driven project aiming to guide established and emerging luxury brands through the complex world of luxury and retail. We provide expertise in therapy services to help brands overcome pressure points and bring finesse to their products and services. We bring a wealth of exposure, experience, and creativity to every project, crafting compelling and viable retail and brand solutions in collaboration with talented industry experts.

Priya Gaekwar

Priya, the in-house strategic powerhouse, leads the Lighthouse team with expansive experience in luxury marketing, having worked with leading global brands and numerous luxury retail projects. With seventeen years in the luxury industry, including managing retail operations at the Louis Vuitton store in Mumbai and serving as the Retail Sales Director at Mumbai’s first multi-brand fashion store, Le Mill, she has developed a specialised understanding of India's luxury market. Originally trained as an interior designer at the Inchbald School of Design in London, Priya crafts pragmatic and successful business plans, providing overall strategies to guide projects to completion. Her attention to detail and creative decision-making set her apart. Outside of work, she indulges her artistic side through painting.

TALK TO HER ABOUT: Design at-large, brand positioning and strategy, arts and culture, mid-century modern chairs, gut health and microbiomes, tri colour beagles.
